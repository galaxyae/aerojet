Services



AERO JET AVIATION SERVICE PROVIDES HELICOPTERS, PROPELLER PLANES & LIGHT JETS FOR AIR TAXI CHARTERS

  • Helicopters: 
We can charter helicopters to access remote locations with limited infrastructure – ideal for charters to mines and rigs.

  • Propeller Planes: 
From piston propellers that can fly up to four passengers to turboprops for groups of up to eight people, propeller planes can land in small airfields closer to your final destination.
  • Very Light Private Jets: 
For a smooth, fast flight for up to six passengers, a private jet is a flexible short-haul travel option.
